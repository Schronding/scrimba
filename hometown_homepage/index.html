Enjoy the town that became city!

Top three activities to do in Queretaro!

Visit the acueduct of Queretaro

The acueduct of Queretaro was made by the Marques de la Villa del Villar del Aguila, as a love offer

Visit the temple of Santa Rosa de Viterbo

The temple was finished in 1752. It was a convent for beatas. Designed by Ignacio Mariano de las Casas was the first temple to have a clock of 3 caratulas.

Visit the Museum of Art

The building was used previously as a cuartel and federal palace, but now it displays art from the XVII to the XIX century.
